Working as one of two cider production apprentices for Heineken in their very first cider production apprentice scheme. I operated the filtering aspect to cider making as well as blending. Blending was the aspect of taking the filtered base blend and adding ingredients to make different ciders, such as Strongbow dark fruits, Bulmers Red and Old Mout.
